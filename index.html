Do you need staff?

Maybe your company is facing a major expansion, a work stoppage or an unexpected vacancy. If this is the case, ProTeam Bemanning is the answer. We provide experienced staff in warehousing, long-distance transport, shaft, demountable trucks, crane trucks, garbage and garbage industry.

ProTeam as a haulier

Since 2019, we also run the company as a haulier, driving around the clock, always on time. ProTeam Bemanning owns 5 tractors that drive freight transport throughout Sweden.

Available jobs 10.10.2021

Are you looking to find a new, exciting career in the field of trucking? We are hiring 1 licensed truck driver.

Job responsabilites

  • Drive long distances to deliver goods to customers.
  • Ensuring goods are safely secured.
  • Load and unload cargo.
  • Record cargo deliveries.
  • Keep an activities log and log hours.
  • Positive attitude with customers.
  • Keeping your vehicle in good condition.
  • Inspect truck and record issues.

Job requirements

  • Class A CDL or commercial driver license.
  • Being at least 21 years old.
  • Clean driving record.
  • Meet medical qualifications
  • Pass drug and alcohol test.
  • A high school diploma.
  • Knowledge of English and Swedish language.
